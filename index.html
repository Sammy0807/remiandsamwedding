How it all started

Our Love Story

  • How We Met

    We met while in graduate school in different universities. We were introduced to each other by a mutual relative. Initially, we lived in different countries, but there was an instant connection. We spoke on the phone often and got closer. Sam relocated to Houston. Remi later accepted a clinical fellowship position in Houston and the relationship blossomed. Finally we were together and didn't have to do a long distance relationship anymore. With time, we grew closer and our love flourished to where we are today. We thank God for our union and our immense gratitude goes to our beloved friends and family who God used to make this happen.

  • First Date

    After Remi came to Houston with her mom for Peter and Kelsey's wedding, the couple agreed to see each other more often since they were both in the same country now. Earlier that year, Sam flew to Norfolk, VA and they went out to see Loni Love's comedy show, Sam visited Remi's school, met a few of her colleagues and they had a good time catching up.

  • The Proposal

    Sam invited Remi’s siblings to Houston. A few months prior, Remi’s mother had already relocated to Houston after accepting a new job here. It was nice to be surrounded by our families. Sam devised a way to bring Remi to Herman Park in downtown Houston. It was the couple’s favorite park where they always take evening walks. Next to the beautiful fountain, Sam got down on his knee and proposed. Sam had secretly prepared a party with some family and friends who eventually popped out to suprise Remi as soon as she said "YES!".
